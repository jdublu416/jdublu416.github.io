How to make A Porkroll Sandwich

The best sandwich you'll ever have!

First thing one should do is hunt for porkroll at the grocery store! This will be a challenge here in Virginia since it has gone by the wayside as a popular breakfast meat.

  1. Put two slices of bread in the toaster to your desired darkness; butter.
  2. Take porkroll slice (I use two) in frying pan on medium heat
  3. Fry one egg to over medium
  4. Assemble sandwich with all three ingredients
  5. I add dijon mustard for my condiment in lieu of butter! Amazing flavor marriage...
